# Company Profile — Green Partners

## Overview

Green Partners is a Romanian environmental consultancy providing integrated services in environment, climate change, energy, waste management, and municipal infrastructure. The company works with international financial institutions (EBRD, World Bank, EIB, IFC), EU institutions, UN agencies, bilateral donors (GIZ), and national/local governments. Green Partners is accredited by the Romanian Ministry of Environment for Environmental Impact Assessment and Due Diligence.

## Focus Areas

- Environmental impact assessment and auditing
- Climate change and GHG management
- Waste management and circular economy
- Energy and renewable energy
- Municipal infrastructure (water, wastewater, district heating)
- Nature conservation and biodiversity
- Economic and financial analysis for environmental projects
- Policy analysis and institutional support

## Preferred Regions

- Romania
- Eastern Europe
- Western Balkans
- Central Asia
- Sub-Saharan Africa

## Typical Engagement Profile

- Budget range: €50,000 – €2,000,000
- Team size: 3–10 experts
